About Gorny Ramsay

Welcome to Gorny Ramsay, where passion for burgers meets culinary excellence. Founded in 2020, our restaurant is dedicated to serving the finest flame-grilled burgers crafted from the freshest ingredients.

Our Story

Gorny Ramsay started as a dream to bring back the classic American burger experience with a modern twist. Inspired by the great chefs of the world, we named our restaurant after the legendary Gordon Ramsay, but with a fun, approachable vibe.

Every burger is made to order, using premium beef, fresh vegetables, and house-made sauces. We're committed to quality, sustainability, and making every meal memorable.
